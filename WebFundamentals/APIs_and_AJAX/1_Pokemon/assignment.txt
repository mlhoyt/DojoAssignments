Assignment: Pokemon

Create a website that has all the pictures of the original Pokemon:

    Pokemon #1 ~ Pokemon #151

Use the PokeAPI to get the images. You can find the images:

    first Pokemon on http://pokeapi.co/media/img/1.png
    second Pokemon on http://pokeapi.co/media/img/2.png
    third Pokemon on http://pokeapi.co/media/img/3.png
    and so on...

You can list out 151 images or you can use a for loop (use a for loop).
